Who am I?


Hi there!
My name is Victoria, and I'm a tech student for the performing arts. This means I get to spend my time plugging cables into outlets, programming lighting designs, and blasting music through a sound system.

I also do hair and makeup designing for the shows performed on campus. I've made a college student pass for a senior citizen, and drawn fake tattoos that were so convincing, even people with tattoos thought they were real.
One of the reasons they looked real is because I used a green sharpie, as opposed to a black one, since a green hued ink is common in slightly older tattoos. The link below explains this. Tattoos are actually green!
